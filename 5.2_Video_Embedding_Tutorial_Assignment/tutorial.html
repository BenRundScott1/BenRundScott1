How To Embed A Video1

The first step in embedding a video is clearing a space for it

The second step is to go to your service-hosted video platform. (We're using Youtube in this example). In the options is an option to share.

The third step is to open the embed option and copy the code provided, that begins and ends with iframe tags.

The fourth step is to paste that code into the body section, and voila. Your video should now display!
