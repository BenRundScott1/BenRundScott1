The Great Rotary Asian Noodle Cookoff

Do you like noodles? Do you like money? Do you like supporting small businesses? Well lucky for you, Holland Rotary Afterhours is now announcing the Great Rotary Asian Noodle Cookoff!

Where?


Midtown Center
96 West 15th Street

When?


5-9pm

Come on out to eat noodles from a variety of Asian restaurants in the area! 11 small business restaurants are competing for your votes, and the winner will be crowned at the end of the night. Entry is only $10 per person

We're so excited for this project! To be able to bring our community together in celebration of the cultures that make up our town is a privilege we're honored to have.
-Joe Jander, Afterhours Rotary Chair
